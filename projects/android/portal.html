Portal — Mobile Login to Web Admin Panel

Purpose: Internal learning project developed during my time at Exnor Soft, aimed at gaining hands-on experience with app development and API integration.

Concept: An Android application that allows users to connect to the websites they own by logging in with their credentials. These are stored locally to maintain an active session, and users can switch between their websites at any time. The app uses a WebView to display the selected site.

Technologies: Android Studio, Java, Volley, SQLite, Material Design 3, Firebase Messaging, WebView

Collaboration: Developed as part of a team. I was responsible for building the Android application and coordinating with colleagues who had developed the websites and APIs. Their support helped ensure smooth integration between the app and backend services.

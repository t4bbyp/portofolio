In progress: Rent Manager

Purpose: Personal learning project to explore and apply a wide range of Android development features.

Concept: An Android application tailored for the real estate sector, designed primarily for property owners. The app allows users to register and manage properties—including buildings and land—by inputting key details such as price, status (rent/sale), area, number of rooms, and address. A dedicated map page integrates the Google Maps Platform to visually display property locations. Additionally, the app includes a Contacts section for managing client information (name, phone number, email). customers (phone number, email, name).

Technologies: Android Studio, Java, Volley, MD3, REST APIs (PHP), MySQL, ngrok, Google Maps Platform.

Planned Features:

  • Link contacts (clients) to their respective properties.
  • Upload and view property images and related documents.
  • Separate login system for clients.
  • Rent payment system integrated into the app.
  • Payment logs for tracking transaction history
  • In-app chat functionality between owners and clients
